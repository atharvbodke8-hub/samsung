samsung tv neo



Samsung Neo QLED 4K TV offers an array of great features to enhance your viewing experience! It includes Quantum Matrix Technology,
Neural Quantum Processor 4K, Dolby Atmos®, EyeComfort Mode, OTS, and much more.
Key Technologies:
1)Quantum Matrix Technology (Mini LED Backlighting): This is the core of Neo QLED. The traditional, larger LEDs are replaced by tens of thousands of much smaller Mini LEDs
(around 1/40th the size of conventional LEDs). This allows for many more local dimming zones, providing ultra-fine and precise control over brightness and contrast,
resulting in deeper blacks and brighter whites.
2)Quantum Dot Technology: Like standard QLED TVs, Neo QLED uses a layer of Quantum Dots to produce a billion shades of vibrant and accurate colors, delivering 100% color volume.
3)NQ AI Processor: Powered by numerous AI neural networks (the latest models use NQ4 AI Gen3 or NQ8 AI Gen3 processors, depending on 4K/8K resolution),
these chips enhance both picture and sound quality in real-time. A key feature is AI Upscaling, which can take lower-resolution content and enhance it to near-4K or even 8K quality.
4)Anti-Glare & Wide Viewing Angle: Many Neo QLED models feature a "Glare Free" screen designed to reduce reflections from ambient light, along with a wide viewing
angle so the picture quality holds up well even when viewed from the side.
5)Object Tracking Sound (OTS): This audio technology uses multiple built-in speakers (e.g., side and up-firing) to create a dynamic, 3D-like sound experience where
the audio moves along with the on-screen action.
