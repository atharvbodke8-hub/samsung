Samsung is a South Korean multinational conglomerate founded in 1938,
known globally as a leader in electronics and technology, with a diverse business portfolio and a leading position in the smartphone market
1)Mobile Devices: This includes a wide range of smartphones (Galaxy S, Z, A series), tablets (Galaxy Tab), wearables (Galaxy Watch, Buds), and accessories.
2)Consumer Electronics: Samsung manufactures TVs (QLED, OLED), home appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners), and computer monitors.
3)Device Solutions: The company is a major producer of components such as memory, semiconductor chips, storage technologies (SSDs), and display panels.
4)Software & Services: Samsung offers a suite of software and services, including Bixby, Samsung Pay,
SmartThings (IoT platform), Samsung Health, and the secure Knox platform.
